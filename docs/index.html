About Me

As a highly motivated and dedicated developer, I possess a diverse skill set in web technologies, including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, and PHP. Currently, I am expanding my expertise by learning Laravel and Vue.js. I am employed at Purbani Group as an Assistant Officer-IT (Software Development), where I previously served as an intern.

In 2021, I earned my BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from American International University Bangladesh, followed by an MSc in Computer Science from Jahangirnagar University in 2023.

My passion lies in continuously learning about new technologies and applying them to drive improvements for both society and my country.
